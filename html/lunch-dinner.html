Know your spices

Black pepper

A versatile spice that is a staple in every kitchen worldwide but did you know that it is high in antioxidant and has anti-inflammatory properties.

Cardamom

Known for its complex flavour, cardamom is used in curries, desserts and baked good. It is believed to be beneficial for diabetes and heart health.

Ginger

Particularly used in Asian cuisines, ginger contains anti-inflammatory properties and is traditionally used for nausea relief.

Garlic

Garlic is a good source of several nutrients such as manganese, vitamin B6, vitamin C, selenium and fiber. Also it can helps protect from illness including common cold.

Cumin

Cumin is the aromatic seeds of the parsley family. It is known to promote normal digestion and is a natural rich source of iron.
