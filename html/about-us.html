About Us

This website has been developed as part of the Secure Font-End Application module for Cyber Security course at University of Technology Mauritius (UTM). It's main obbjective is providing its readers with the simplest possible and easy to make recipes.

There are also some tips and tricks that beginners can learn from to help you become a star in your kitchen.

Yes, we know. There are already so many recipe websites out there. But hey! Think of it this way, now you have one more variation of a recipe that you have been looking for!

Each recipe pages (atleast the few that there are) jumps straight to the point, that is the ingredients and instructions. Not much story-telling going on. That's because we believe that the readers need to tell their own story through their cooking.
