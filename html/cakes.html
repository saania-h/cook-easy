FAQs: What went wrong with my cake?

Why is my cake cracked on top?

Overmixing, high oven temperature or too much flour. Adjust the oven temperature according to your recipe. Mix your batter until just well combined. Finally, ensure proper flour measurement; use an electronic balance for a more accurate measurement.

Why is my cake sticking to the pan?

Pan is not greased enough, cake is not cool enough before removing from pan. Make sure to grease your pan well or align it with parchment paper. Allow the cake to cool for about 10 minutes before inverting it onto a wire rack.

Why is my cake so dense and chewy?

Overmixing! Overmixing the batter causes the flour to release gluten which makes the cake chewy, dense and causes the cake to take longer to cook.

So, add the flour little by little and mix until just combined.
