Classic Vanilla Cupcakes

You have been looking for the most moist cupcake recipe? Well, this might be your last stop because this is a tried and proven recipe straight out of my grade 8 home economics book. For beginners, it may look a bit complex but believe me, this is one of the easiest recipes there is.

See what people are saying about this recipe!

Prep time

20 minutes

Cook time

20 minutes

Servings

8

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredients
100g Soft margarine
100g Sugar
2 Eggs
125g Self-raising flour
1/4 tsp Vanilla essence

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven at 190°C
  2. Place your paper cases into patty tins.
  3. Cream the margarine and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. In a seperate bowl, crack the 2 eggs and beat until well mixed. Add a little at a time beating well at each. You may use an electric mixer or a wooden spoon.
  5. Then, gradually fold in the flour using a spatula. Add the vanilla essence. Mix until everything is well combined.
  6. Divide the mixture between the paper cases and smooth the top.
  7. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes until well risen, firm and golden brown.
  8. Optional: You can add some sprinkles on top before putting it into the oven.

The infinite possiblities

A little reminder!

Remember not to overmix your batter when adding flour or else you will get a chewy cupcake. Nobody wants that!
